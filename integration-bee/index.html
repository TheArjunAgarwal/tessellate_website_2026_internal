The CMI Integration Bee is an annual tournament held at the Chennai Mathematical Institute during Tessellate.

The event is open to all CMI students, from first-year undergraduates to PhD scholars, post-docs, and professors.

This year, it is open to external participants.

üìò Competition Format (2026)

  • 01. Qualification Round: A 30-minute written exam featuring 8-10 integrals.
  • 02. The Top 16: The highest scorers from the qualifier advance to the live tournament stage.
  • 03. Tournament Stage: An elimination bracket where participants face off at the chalkboard.
  • 04. Prizes: All Qualifiers receive prizes donated by our esteemed faculty members.
